# Pilot Plan (Private Review)

This plan describes a small, private review of the draft site and documentation.
It is ethics-first, non-activating, and aligned with voluntary participation.

## Purpose
- Validate clarity, neutrality, and readability of the draft materials.
- Identify any language that could be interpreted as activating or persuasive.
- Confirm alignment with `/ethics`, `docs/FREEZE_POLICY.md`, and communications rules.

## Scope
- Draft site pages (content only).
- Draft documentation set in `docs/`.
- No onboarding, claims, wallets, or incentives.

## Participants (Private)
- A small, invited group with diverse perspectives.
- Participation is voluntary, with no expectation of continued involvement.

## Process
1. Provide the draft materials and the ethics checklist.
2. Ask reviewers to note unclear, ambiguous, or activation-leaning language.
3. Collect feedback in a neutral format (no ranking or scoring).
4. Review feedback internally and record decisions in the changelog.

## Deliverables
- A short summary of findings.
- A list of approved wording adjustments, if any.
- Confirmation that no activating features are present.

## Safeguards
- No data collection beyond the feedback itself.
- No urgency, reward, or access implications.
- Reviewers can disengage at any time without penalty.
