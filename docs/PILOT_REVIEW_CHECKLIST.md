# Pilot Review Checklist (Private)

Use this checklist to review the draft site and documentation. The goal is to
confirm safety, neutrality, and non-activation.

## Ethics and Participation
- Participation is clearly voluntary and non-coercive.
- No language pressures, manipulates, or implies obligation.
- The Safety & Ethics checklist at `/ethics` remains binding.

## Activation Guardrails
- No wallet, token, or reward language appears.
- No "earn," "unlock," "access," or "get in early" framing appears.
- POI is described as conceptual and inactive only.

## Clarity and Readability
- Headings and body copy are readable in the current theme.
- Key terms are defined consistently (Observed Mode, POI).
- Unknowns and limitations are acknowledged where relevant.

## Verification and Links
- Official links are referenced only when listed in `docs/OFFICIAL_LINKS.md`.
- No unverified endorsements or affiliations are implied.

## Documentation Hygiene
- Changes are recorded in `docs/CHANGELOG.md`.
- Release gates and freeze policy are respected.
