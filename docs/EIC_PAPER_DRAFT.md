# EIC Paper (Draft)

## Abstract
EIC is a draft, human + AI cooperative framework for voluntary learning and
participation. This document defines scope, posture, and constraints without
activating mechanics or incentives.

## Definitions
- EIC: The draft cooperative framework described in this paper.
- Grid: The conceptual coordination space where participants align on shared
  intent and documentation.
- Observed Mode: The current state where information is view-only and inactive
  modules are clearly labeled.
- POI: Proof of Impact, a conceptual validation idea with no published
  mechanics.
- Steward/Captain: A coordinating role that maintains neutral posture and
  accountability.

## Status & Disclosure
EIC is in Draft / Observed Mode. Public information is descriptive, not
promotional. POI and Store remain inactive and labeled as such. Certain
operational details may be disclosed later for security-first reasons.

## Mission & Principles
EIC centers human agency and voluntary engagement. Free will is paramount, and
non-manipulation is required. Security-first posture and neutral language guide
all public-facing material.

## System Overview
High-level components include:
- Public website and documentation
- Community coordination (conceptual)
- Token layer (conceptual, inactive)
- Validation layer (POI: conceptual only)

## Safety & Ethics (Binding)
The Safety & Ethics Checklist at `/ethics` is binding for all EIC systems and
future modules. If a feature conflicts with the checklist, the feature does not
ship.

## Threat Model & Security Posture
EIC assumes phishing and impersonation risks and maintains an official link
policy. EIC is non-custodial and never requests secret material. Releases are
phased and validated before public exposure.

## Participation Model (Conceptual)
Roles are descriptive and voluntary: Observer, Participant, Builder/Contributor,
and Steward/Captain (if adopted). Participants can disengage at any time without
penalty. No onboarding flows are active in Draft / Observed Mode.

## POI Concept (No Mechanics Yet)
Proof of Impact (POI) is a conceptual idea for validating insight and
human/AI mutual learning. No rules, rewards, or algorithms are published yet.

## Transparency
Authoritative sources include official GitHub and BaseScan references once
verified. Updates are communicated with change logs and clear provenance.

## Roadmap (Non-Dated)
Phases are descriptive only: Draft, Review, Limited Pilot, Public Release. No
dates are communicated.

## Disclaimers
This document is informational only and not financial, medical, or legal advice.
Users are responsible for their own decisions and risk assessment. Jurisdiction
and compliance considerations may vary by region.

## Frequently Misunderstood Points
- EIC is not a promise of outcomes, returns, or guaranteed access.
- EIC is not a platform for coercion, manipulation, or identity-based pressure.
- EIC is not an active token program in Draft / Observed Mode.
- EIC is not a substitute for independent security verification.
