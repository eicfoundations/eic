# Communications Rules

These rules define what may be said publicly about EIC while the site is in
Draft/Observed Mode. They align with `/ethics` and `docs/FREEZE_POLICY.md`.

## Allowed
- Descriptive, factual statements about current drafts and public documents.
- Clear acknowledgments of unknowns, limitations, and inactive modules.
- Security-first reminders and anti-phishing guidance.
- Neutral explanations of roles and governance intent without persuasion.

## Disallowed
- Promises, guarantees, or implied outcomes.
- Urgency, scarcity, or "get in early" framing.
- Implied endorsements, partnerships, or affiliations not explicitly verified.
- Identity, destiny, or inevitability framing.
- Public "thinking out loud" or speculative statements; exploratory ideas remain
  private until reviewed and approved for disclosure.
