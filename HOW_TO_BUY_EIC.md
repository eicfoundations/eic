## Overview

Welcome! This document explains how to acquire and trade **Energy Intelligence Coin (EIC)** on the **Base Mainnet** network.

EIC is a decentralized token with liquidity available via the Aerodrome DEX. There is **no centralized sale portal** at this time ‚Äî trading is done entirely through decentralized markets.

## 1. Wallet Requirements

To buy or trade EIC, you must use a crypto wallet that supports the **Base** network.

Examples:
- MetaMask
- Coinbase Wallet
- Rabby Wallet

If you are bridging from Ethereum Mainnet, bridge your ETH into **Base** before swapping.  
Wallet must be connected to the Base network in order to transact.

## 3. Method A ‚Äî Swap for EIC

To acquire EIC:

1. Go to the Aerodrome DEX interface on Base.
2. Connect your wallet.
3. Set the swap parameters:
   - **From:** WETH
   - **To:** EIC
4. Adjust slippage tolerance as needed.
5. Confirm and submit the transaction.

After the transaction completes, you will see your EIC balance in your wallet.

## 4. Method B ‚Äî Provide Liquidity (Advanced)

If you want to participate deeper:

1. Supply **equal values** of WETH and EIC into the liquidity pool.
2. Deposit them into the **WETH/EIC pool**.
